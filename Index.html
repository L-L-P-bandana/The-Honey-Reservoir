About My Daring Self

In all the years spent on this here planet Earth, nothing has quite motivated my rabid caffine addiction quite like losing myself in some coding problem, only to then cast hours upon days into void, in a rabit pursuit of making whatever I've solved into a decked out super project.

Naturaly, as I aged, making money became a thing, and as luck would have it, people let me know that they appreciated ("some of") my little autism projects, and have done so by graciously throwing some money my way in a hopes that fueling my code junkie lifestyle would leave them with a sparkling diamond in it's wake.

If you would like your very own sparkling code diamond, then I'd be honored to work with you, and I'll do my gosh darn bestest to make your ideas pierce the heavens in immaculate splendor.
